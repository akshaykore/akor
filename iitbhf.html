IIT Bombay Heritage Fund
IIT Bombay Heritage Fund Branding and Website
Design and Brand Guidelines for the IITB Heritage fund website
Collaborators : Rohan Verma
Guide : Prof. Anirudha Joshi
The IITBHF is a network of IIT Bombay's alumni established to assist the institute. As a part of the teaching assistantship under Prof. Anirudha Joshi at IDC, Rohan and I were asked to decide the branding and style guidelines for the IIT Bombay's Heritage Fund (IITBHF) website.
