Vayu
Vayu
An Interactive installation for India HCI 2014 at IIT Delhi
Collaborators : Interaction Design 2014-16 IDC IIT Bombay
Vayu, a performative installation was created for the India HCI Conference held at IIT Delhi in 2014 by the Interaction Design batch of 2014-16 from IDC IITB. It is an arduino based installation which uses the analogy of blowing to spin a pinwheel. In this case the visitor is able to spin 15 pinwheels when blowing into a single microphone, thereby augmenting the visitors' abilities.
